    Oh, hi there!

    I’m a human-loving introvert based in a cold, northern country in my hometown Trondheim.

    I have a bachelor degree in Graphic Design from Kristiania University College, majoring in web- and universal design from my bachelor’s thesis. After first semester when I was introduced to interaction design subject and the coding languages like HTML & CSS for building websites, it led me after graduation into the Front -end Development program at Noroff Vocational school of technology and digital media where I further worked toward my UX/UI path and dream. As a designer, I like to keep it clean and simple, where my goals in every project is to focus on transparent content and clear visual communication that works across different devices and media. Ever since I was a kid, I’ve always wanted to work in a creative field that involves design that could possibly make the digital world a better and more beautiful place to live in.
